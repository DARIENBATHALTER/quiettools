Student Analytics Platform

WASABI

Transform raw student data into actionable insights with this fully cusomizable, comprehensive analytics platform built specifically for educators.

The Wide Array Student Analytics and Benchmarking Interface (WASABI) helps school administrators track academic progress, attendance patterns, and intervention needs across diverse educational ecosystems.

Key Features

WASABI makes complex student data accessible and actionable.

Comprehensive Analytics

Track student performance across multiple metrics with intuitive dashboards and reports that make patterns visible at a glance.

Attendance Tracking

Monitor attendance patterns and identify at-risk students before problems escalate. Set automatic alerts for concerning trends.

Professional Insights

Track what works and what doesn't with WASABI's companion app, SOBA, for taking real-time classroom observation data and delivering targeted feedback on student engagement to teachers.

Intervention Management

Plan, document, and track interventions for struggling students. See what works and coordinate support across teaching teams.

FERPA Compliance

Built with privacy and security in mind, WASABI works entirely offline or on your school's local network. All student data is protected according to federal education privacy standards.

WASABI in Action

Here are a few examples of WASABI dashboards, which can be customized to show different metrics and data for different grade levels and classes.

Student Profile Cards - display key metrics and data for each student, and can be exported directly to PDF or printed.
Growth Insights - WASABI can be adapted to recieve student data in virtually any format, and enables detailed student progress tracking over time
Customizable Grade Level and Class Analytics Dashboards - Create tailored views for different grade levels and classes, allowing administrators to focus on the metrics that matter most for each group.
Customizable and Exportable Student Reports - Generate detailed reports for individual students or groups, with flexible formatting options for sharing with parents, teachers, and administrators.
