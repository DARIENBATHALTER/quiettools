Key Features

SOBA elevates teacher evaluation to a growth-oriented experience.

Laptop or Touch Friendly

Take digital notes during observations with intuitive touch-based forms designed for tablets and mobile devices. No more paper shuffling or post-observation transcription.

Growth Tracking

Give prompt and specific feedback to educators, and keep track of what works. Monitor teacher development over time with visual progress indicators. Identify growth patterns and celebrate improvement with concrete data.

Trend Analysis

Identify school-wide patterns and professional development needs with powerful analytics that reveal strengths and opportunities across your faculty.

Custom Frameworks

Adapt SOBA to any teaching framework or rubric your school uses, from Danielson to Marzano or your own custom evaluation criteria.
