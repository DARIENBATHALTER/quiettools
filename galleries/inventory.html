Mobile Inventory Scanner

MISO

Turn any smartphone into a powerful inventory management system with zero complexity.

The Mobile Inventory Scanner & Organizer (MISO) transforms how schools, small businesses, and organizations manage their assets and inventory with professional-grade features in an intuitive mobile experience.

Key Features

Professional inventory management without enterprise complexity.

Smart Barcode Scanning

Instantly scan any standard barcode or QR code using your smartphone camera. No additional hardware required.

Real-Time Updates

Synchronize inventory data across all devices in real-time. Never deal with conflicting information or outdated records again.

In-Depth Asset Management

Supports multiple custom label formats, and metadata for electronic devices, like make model condition OS serial and user assignment.

Usage Analytics

Track consumption patterns, identify trends, and optimize your ordering process with built-in analytics.

Who Uses MISO

Perfect for organizations that need professional inventory tracking without enterprise complexity.

K-12 Schools of All Sizes

From IT equipment to classroom supplies, MISO helps schools track their assets and manage resource allocation efficiently. Easily manage textbooks, technology, and equipment with a simple scanning system that staff can learn in minutes.

Small Businesses

Retail shops, restaurants, and service businesses use MISO to manage inventory without the overhead of complex systems. Keep track of stock levels, automate reordering, and understand your inventory flow with intuitive analytics.
