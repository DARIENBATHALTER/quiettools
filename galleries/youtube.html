YouTube Comment Insights

MOCHI

Generate months of social content in minutes with authentic audience insights.

MOCHI helps content creators understand their audience by automatically gathering and analyzing YouTube comments. Turn thousands of comments into viral-ready screenshots and strategic content direction. The built-in word frequency analysis reveals trending topics and audience interests, while the professional export feature creates content for videos, thumbnails, and social media.

Key Features

Generate thousands of comment screenshots in minutes.

Channel Data Scraping

Automatically scrapes YouTube for an updated list of videos from any channel, including all comments, like counts, and reply comments.

Advanced Comment Filtering

Powerful search and filtering capabilities to find specific comments, sentiment patterns, or engagement levels across your entire channel history.

High-Frequency Word Analysis

Identify trending topics and common themes in your comments with word frequency analysis to gain valuable audience insights.

Screenshot Export

Export comments individually or in bulk as realistic YouTube screenshots for use in content creation, thumbnails, and social media.
