Podcast Review Analytics

Re-Viewer

Create simulated screenshots from comments instantly. Unlock powerful insights from your podcast reviews and feedback.

Re-Viewer does a few things well: it scrapes all comments from Apple Podcast, includes advanced review sort/filtering, keyword insights, and export in a variety of realistic screenshot simulations.

Key Features

Everything you need to understand and showcase your podcast reviews.

Apple Podcast Scraping

Automatically scrapes all reviews and comments from Apple Podcasts for comprehensive analysis of your show's feedback.

Advanced Review Filtering

Sort and filter reviews by rating, date, keyword, sentiment, and more to find exactly the feedback you're looking for.

Keyword Insights

Identify trending topics and common themes in your reviews with powerful keyword analysis to understand what listeners love most.

Screenshot Simulations

Export reviews in a variety of realistic screenshot formats including simulated iPhone screenshots and macOS screenshots for content creation.
